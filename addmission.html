Admission Process

We welcome applications for the upcoming academic year. Follow these steps to apply:

1. Inquiry

Submit an inquiry form online or visit our campus.

2. Application

Complete the application form with required documents.

3. Assessment

Students may be required to take an assessment test.

4. Decision

Admission decisions are communicated within 2 weeks.
